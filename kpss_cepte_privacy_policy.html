Privacy Policy

KPSS Cepte Mobile Application

Welcome to KPSS Cepte ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This helps us improve the App's features and performance.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports, which help us identify and fix bugs in the App.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, such as the model and operating system version, to ensure compatibility and improve the App.
  • Advertising ID: The App may display advertisements and, in doing so, collect your advertising ID to provide relevant advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Local Data: The App stores your test progress, scores, and preferences locally on your device using the Room database. This data is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

We do not collect personal information such as your name or email address unless you choose to contact us directly via email.

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To improve the App's functionality and user experience.
  • To fix bugs and ensure the App runs smoothly.
  • To provide relevant advertisements.
  • To save your progress and preferences locally on your device.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With Firebase and Google AdMob, as described above, to provide analytics, crash reporting, and advertisements.
  • If required by law or to protect our rights.

Please note that these third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. Additionally, your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country, where data protection laws may differ.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Local data is stored securely on your device.
  • Data transmitted to third-party services is sent over secure connections (HTTPS).

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Control: You can access and manage your local data within the App. Uninstalling the App will delete all local data.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings or resetting your advertising ID.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
