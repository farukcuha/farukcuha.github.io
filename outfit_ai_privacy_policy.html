Privacy & Data Policy

Overview

Welcome to Outfit AI, developed by Ahmet Faruk Çuha. We’re not here to sugar-coat things: your privacy is our responsibility. This document details the data we collect, how we use it, and the robust measures we have in place to protect your information.

1. Data Collection

A. Information You Actively Provide

  • Visual Inputs: Photos and images you upload for virtual try-on features.
  • User Settings & Preferences: Your chosen style settings and outfit configurations.
  • Transaction Details: Purchase records, subscription statuses, and payment information.

B. Data Gathered Automatically

  • Device Metrics: Device type, OS details, unique identifiers, and network data.
  • Usage Analytics: App performance data, error logs, and engagement statistics used to improve the service.

C. Permissions We Require

  • Access to Photos: To select and store your try-on images securely.
  • Camera Permissions: Allowing direct photo captures within the app.
  • Notification Permissions: For timely alerts on process completions, updates, and exclusive offers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data powers the core of Outfit AI:

  • Core Functionality: Processing your virtual try-on requests, managing your history, and handling in-app transactions.
  • Continuous Improvement: Using analytics to fine-tune our app, fix bugs, and personalize your experience.
  • Communications: With your consent, sending you updates, special offers, and new feature announcements.

We integrate advanced machine learning to analyze anonymized trends, ensuring that our service evolves with your needs.

3. Data Storage & Security Measures

A. Local Storage

  • Your try-on images and data are cached on your device for quick access and are securely managed.
  • Automatic cleanup routines keep storage optimized without compromising your privacy.

B. Cloud Storage

  • We rely on Apple’s CloudKit for secure data storage, ensuring compliance with strict privacy standards.
  • All data transfers and storage are encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

Our security framework is designed to defend against unauthorized access, ensuring your data remains confidential and intact.

4. Third-Party Integrations

To deliver an exceptional service, we partner with trusted third-party providers:

  • RevenueCat: Oversees in-app purchase transactions and subscription management securely.
  • FashnAPI: Handles image processing for virtual try-ons, ensuring temporary data is purged immediately post-processing.

All third-party partners are vetted to comply with high data protection standards.

5. Data Retention Policy

We retain your data based on necessity and legal mandates:

  • Try-On History: Stored locally with options for manual deletion at any time.
  • Analytical & Cache Data: Periodically purged to maintain optimal performance and relevance.
  • Transaction Records: Maintained as required by law and accounting standards.

6. Your Rights & Data Control

You’re in charge of your data. Our policy ensures you can:

  • Access and review your personal data stored within the app.
  • Delete your try-on history and any stored images.
  • Adjust or revoke notification and permission settings via your device.
  • Request data deletion or further information by contacting us directly.

We act promptly on such requests, adhering to legal frameworks and your rights.

7. Children’s Privacy

Outfit AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If such data is inadvertently collected, we will delete it without delay.

This Privacy & Data Policy may evolve as our service grows. We will notify you of any significant changes via in-app alerts or email. Continued use of Outfit AI constitutes acceptance of any modifications.
